MANDALAS
p5js | 2020
Mandalas, in South Asian cultures, are considered to be tools of guidance and focus in conjunction with the practice of meditation. A mandala’s circular composition connotes that, “Life is never ending and everything in the universe is connected”. It can put you in a trance-like state and help you connect with yourself in a deeper way. I chose to use this project to create interactive mandalas that the viewer can enjoy, with the colors and movements of their virtual limb around the screen as the mandala expands and contracts, almost imitating our breathing patterns. I hope they can use this digital interaction as an escape from their daily routine, and find a way to ease their mind and centre themselves whenever they please.
